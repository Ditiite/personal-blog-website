Story how it's started

While I was sitting home in parental leave with my second baby, I was looking for some interesting nursery decorations. Some pieces from macrame catch my eyes. I decided to try out. I found this beautiful tutorial, ordered cord and tried it out. It was like meditation. Although it wasn't easy to start, I found it so relaxing and soon I was addicted to macrame. I wasted to try out more and more things to make. Now it's may hobby and I love it.


I am so glad I decided to knot my first knot and didn't scared of trying something new. As I have found the way tor relax my brain after stressful and rush full day.
